Founded in 2012, Sunrisers Hyderabad replaced Deccan Chargers. Known for strong bowling units and disciplined strategy, SRH won IPL in 2016 under David Warner.

Achievements

  • IPL Champions - 2016
  • Multiple Playoff Appearances
  • Finalist: Finished as runners-up, reaching their first final since 2018.
  • Record-Breaking Scores: Set the top two highest team totals in IPL history: 287/3 vs RCB and 277/3 vs MI.
  • Aggressive Batting Records: Recorded the highest-ever Powerplay score (125/0) in T20 cricket (against DC).
  • Fastest Run Chase: Achieved the fastest 150+ run chase in T20 history, reaching 166 in just 9.4 overs against LSG.
  • Powerhouse Performers: Abhishek Sharma hit the most sixes by an opener (42), while Travis Head and Heinrich Klaasen provided explosive, record-setting performances.
